Privacy Policy

Iris Lens does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal data.

How the app works

Iris Lens uses the device camera to process images in real time and enhance color visibility through vision modes and contrast adjustments. All image processing happens on-device.

Data collection

Iris Lens does not create user accounts and does not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, location data, contact information, or camera content.

Photos access

If you choose to save a snapshot, the app requests permission only to save the image to your Photos library. Iris Lens does not upload or share saved images unless you explicitly choose to do so through iOS sharing features.

Third-party services

Iris Lens does not use third-party advertising or tracking services.

Your choices

You can revoke camera or photo access at any time in iOS Settings.

FAQ

The camera is not working. What should I do?

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera and make sure Iris Lens has permission to access the camera.

Why can’t I save snapshots?

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos and allow Iris Lens to add photos.

Does Iris Lens work offline?

Yes. Iris Lens works locally on your device and processes images on-device.
